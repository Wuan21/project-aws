> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. FCAJ members are always ready to help whenever I face difficulties, even outside of working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, helping me stay focused. However, I think adding more team bonding events could help everyone understand each other better.

**2. Support from the Admin Team**  
The admin team provides very detailed guidance and always encourages interns to ask questions when encountering issues with system architecture, while also supplying comprehensive learning materials. I particularly appreciate that the admins respond to questions quickly and suggest approaches to help me think through problems independently rather than just handing out ready-made answers.

**3. Alignment Between the Training Program and Academic Major**  
The program's knowledge complements my university studies very well. It not only reinforces foundational theories in networking and databases, but also gives me hands-on experience directly on AWS — practical skills that are rarely accessible in a classroom setting.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I developed many new skills including using project management tools, teamwork, and professional communication in a corporate environment. The speakers also shared valuable real-world experience that helped me better plan my career path.

**5. Team Culture & Spirit**  
The learning culture is very positive: interns are respectful, open-minded, and supportive of each other. When facing complex system design problems or during the final project sprint, everyone actively discusses and shares knowledge. This helped me feel genuinely connected and progress faster throughout the program.

**6. Internship Policies & Benefits**  
Having flexible working hours and access to AWS resources for hands-on practice is an invaluable privilege. The in-depth internal training sessions guided by experts are a benefit that is truly priceless for any intern.


### Additional Questions

- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
Being directly involved in building real-world AWS architecture for a large-scale project (Flash Sale system) rather than just learning theory. The enthusiasm and support from the admin team is something I deeply appreciate.

- **What do you think should be improved for future interns?**  
It would be helpful to have a consolidated reference document with detailed guidelines on system architecture standards. This would help future interns quickly get oriented and standardize their technical diagrams from the very first weeks.

- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why?**  
Absolutely. For me, this is not just a great internship environment — it is a precious opportunity to learn, get familiar with real-world practice, and gain the motivation to keep growing and developing on my career path.
